Earth & Mars

Earth Lush, breathing Whispering, cradling, teeming Forests, oceans — canyons, dust Rusting, orbiting, waiting Barren, distant Mars

Summer & Winter

Summer Golden, humming Blazing, shimmering, blooming Bees, beaches — frost, silence Drifting, hushing, crystallizing Pale, sleeping Winter
